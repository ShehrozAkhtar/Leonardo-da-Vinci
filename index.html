Leonardo da Vinci

Leonardo da Vinci (1452–1519) was an Italian polymath whose diverse talents spanned painting, science, engineering, anatomy, and more. Known primarily for iconic paintings like the *Mona Lisa* and *The Last Supper*, his art demonstrates his deep understanding of anatomy, light, and perspective. Leonardo was also an inventor and a scientist, filling his notebooks with sketches and studies of flying machines, anatomical drawings, and scientific observations that were far ahead of his time.
